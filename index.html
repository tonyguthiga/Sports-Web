GOLF

What is Golf?

Golf is a club and ball sport whereby the players (golfers) attempt to hit the balls into each hole of a golf course with the fewest number of strokes.

Why do we play Golf?

Golfing is great place to meet new people, participate in healthy competition, learn the value of patience and self-improvement and finally but not least it's all about enjoying and having fun.

Categories of Golf Tournament

Golf has 7 types of tournament each with its own purpose and fun experience. They include;

  1. Match play
  2. Stroke play
  3. Best Ball
  4. Scramble
  5. Bingo Bango Bongo
  6. Chapman
  7. Foursome(Alternate shot)
